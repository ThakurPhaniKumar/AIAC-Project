FAQ

Common questions

Is ResumeCraft completely free?
Yes — all core features including AI summary generation, all templates, and PDF download are completely free. No credit card required.
How does the AI summary work?
It reads the job titles, years of experience, and skills you've provided, then generates a concise, professional summary tailored to your profile.
Is my data saved automatically?
Yes — every change is automatically saved to your browser's local storage. Close the tab, reopen it, your data is exactly as you left it.
Are the templates ATS-friendly?
All templates use clean, semantic HTML. Single-column templates are optimized for ATS parsers used by major companies.
