FAQ

Common questions

Is ResumeCraft completely free?
Yes — all core features including AI summary generation, all templates, cover letter generator, and PDF download are completely free. No credit card required.
How does the AI work?
ResumeCraft uses Claude AI (by Anthropic) to generate professional summaries and cover letters. It reads your job titles, years of experience, and skills to craft tailored content.
Can I have multiple resumes?
Yes! Your dashboard lets you create, manage, duplicate, and delete as many resumes as you need — great for applying to different types of roles.
Are the templates ATS-friendly?
All templates use clean, semantic HTML. Single-column templates are optimized for ATS parsers used by major companies.
